Applied psychology

Until now, Artificial Intelligence focused only on logical-rational aspects, but already from the imagination of science fiction, taken to literature or to the cinema, affective-emotional components had been incorporated into the creations robotic. Now, and beyond fiction, this would allow us to increase the effectiveness of intelligent systems in their interaction specific to different needs sentimental human. The robots humanoid, androids (male) or gynoids (female) will have to be programmed with something similar to emotional intelligence to be able to have guidelines of interaction in the communication process with people.
What to do when you smile, or cry? I know express satisfaction or happiness or displeasure? Rage or relaxation? Pain or pleasure? The detection of these reactions would constitute the stimulus model necessary to extract, from the program library behaviors, the most appropriate as Conditioned Response.
